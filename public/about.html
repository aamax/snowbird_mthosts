About the Mountain Host Program


The Mountain Host Program at Snowbird is "Steep in Service and Deep in Information". We are the employees wearing the green coats on the mountain. You will know when you see us because we'll be smiling and eager to help you find what you're looking for, help you if you are hurt, or generally ensure that you have a great experience on our magical mountain.
